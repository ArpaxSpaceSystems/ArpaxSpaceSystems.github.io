ARPAX Space, access to space for amateurs

ARPAX Space is a French amateur space innovation program focused on the design, development, and suborbital launch of a methalox bi-stage rocket. This program encompasses several projects, including the 10-11 kN methalox Panoramix 1A engine, in-house FPGA-based avionics, the reusable Gallus 5 launcher, and educational collaborations such as the integration of student and amateur payloads. The goal is to democratize access to space with low-cost recovery and a payload capacity of up to 100 kg.
